Frequently Asked Questions

What devices does Cue Counter support?

Cue Counter is available for both iOS and Android devices. It's optimized for both phones and tablets to provide the best experience regardless of screen size.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes, Cue Counter works completely offline. All your match data is stored locally on your device. If you choose the Pro or Lifetime plan, you also get the option to back up your data to the cloud when connected to the internet.

What languages are supported?

The free version supports English and Spanish. The Pro and Lifetime versions support all major languages including English, Spanish, Chinese, Hindi, French, and Arabic. We're constantly adding more languages.

Can I switch from monthly to lifetime subscription?

Yes, you can upgrade from the monthly Pro subscription to the Lifetime plan at any time. We'll even provide a prorated discount based on your current subscription.

How does the tournament management work?

The tournament management system allows you to create various tournament formats including knockout, double elimination, round robin, and swiss system. You can add players, generate brackets automatically, track scores, and view comprehensive statistics for the entire tournament.

Is there a desktop version available?

Currently, Cue Counter is only available for mobile devices. However, we're working on a web version that will allow you to view your statistics and manage tournaments from your desktop. This feature will be available to Pro and Lifetime subscribers.
